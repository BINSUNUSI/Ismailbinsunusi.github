ABOUT US

Grand Medic Clinic

is a pioneer in providing Integrated medical care at the highest level, our goal is The Care and safety of patients, through advancing The level of service and the use of the latest technology and global expertise transfer In various medical fields. Being in the line of Healthcare for over 10 years. Have acheived a great deal and named best reowned hospital .Has Medical and Surgical consultants and respective Medical officers and training House officers. Nurses,lab scienties , pharmacist , physiotherapist and many other healthworkers are hardworking and dedicating to serve humanity.

The Clinic for Neurosurgery at Grand Medic includes a group of the most talented surgeons and experts in this subspecialty to provide superior care using the latest surgical equipment and techniques.

The Cardiothoracic Surgery Clinic provides a comprehensive range of services at the hands of the most skilled professors for patients who suffer from heart and chest problems that require surgical intervention.

The Urology Clinic includes a team of distinguished professors to provide excellent medical care to all patients, according to the latest medical findings in the field of kidney and urology surgery.

The orthopedic clinic at Grand Medic has the best professors who specialize in all types of surgeries, equipped with the latest equipment and techniques in the field of orthopedics.

Grand Medic clinic provides all women's health needs regarding diseases of the reproductive system and all hormonal changes starting from puberty.

The Chest Diseases Clinic at Welcare Hospital provides the comprehensive care patients need for treating lung and bronchial diseases, using the latest technology and medical devices.
